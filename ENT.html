Common problems for "Shiro-Roga"/"Shalakya Tantra"[ENT]

  • Sinusitis (Pratishyaya)
  • Allergic Rhinitis (Vataja Pratishyaya)
  • Tonsillitis (Tundikeri or Galaayu)
  • Snoring and Sleep Apnea (Nidraabhishyanda)
  • Hoarseness of Voice (Kantaroga)
  • Ear Infections (Karna Roga)
  • Vertigo (Bhrama)

Common Symptoms

  • Sinusitis (Pratishyaya)

    Congestion, facial pain, headache, nasal discharge, loss of smell, and sometimes fever.

  • Allergic Rhinitis (Vataja Pratishyaya)

    Sneezing, runny nose, nasal congestion, and itchy or watery eyes.

  • Tonsillitis (Tundikeri or Galaayu)

    Sore throat, difficulty swallowing, swollen tonsils, and sometimes fever.

  • Snoring and Sleep Apnea (Nidraabhishyanda)

    Loud snoring, interrupted sleep, excessive daytime sleepiness.

  • Hoarseness of Voice (Kantaroga)

    Changes in voice quality, voice fatigue, and discomfort while speaking.

  • Ear Infections (Karna Roga)

    Ear pain, discharge from the ear, hearing loss, and sometimes fever.

Treatment in Ayurveda

  • Sinusitis (Pratishyaya)

    Nasya therapy with medicated oils, steam inhalation, and Ayurvedic herbs like Tulsi (Holy Basil), Ginger, and Turmeric. Diet should include warm, easily digestible foods.

  • Allergic Rhinitis (Vataja Pratishyaya)

    Avoid allergens, dietary modifications, Triphala powder or Guduchi to boost immunity, and Nasya therapy with medicated oils.

  • Tonsillitis (Tundikeri or Galaayu)

    Gargling with warm saltwater, herbal formulations like Gandhak Rasayana, dietary restrictions on cold and sour foods.

  • Snoring and Sleep Apnea (Nidraabhishyanda)

    Lifestyle changes (weight management, exercise), Ayurvedic formulations like Sitopaladi Churna or Talisadi Churna, and Pranayama.

  • Hoarseness of Voice (Kantaroga)

    Steam inhalation with herbs, gargling with warm water infused with turmeric or licorice, voice rest, and herbal teas.

  • Ear Infections (Karna Roga)

    Warm sesame oil or herbal oils for ear drops, warm compresses, and Ayurvedic formulations like Bilwadi Gutika.

Precautions

  • Avoid cold and damp environments, protect from allergens, maintain nasal hygiene, and stay well-hydrated.
  • Minimize exposure to allergens, practice good hygiene, and follow a balanced diet.
  • Good oral hygiene, avoid cold foods and drinks, and protect the throat from cold winds.
  • Avoid heavy meals before bedtime, maintain a regular sleep schedule, and sleep on your side to reduce snoring
  • Avoid screaming or straining the voice, stay hydrated, and consume soothing herbal teas.
  • Do not insert foreign objects into the ear, keep ears dry and clean, and protect them from cold and wind.
  • Get enough sleep
